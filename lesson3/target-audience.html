Target Audience

The target audience of detailed weather are the people raging from 20-60 years old, who are not expierenced in technology, and who like to go outside. Secondly, the website will be dedicated to the people who like to do travels inside their country.

Two personas

George

  • 25 years old
  • Lives in Charlotte North Carolina
  • Works as a personal trainer in a gym
  • He's is doing a major in Economics
  • He wants to know if he can go to the beach with his friends this weekend, he would like to know a place where he could find an accurate answer for that.

Charlotte

  • 34 years old
  • Lives in Meza Arizona
  • Mom of three children
  • Likes to read, and enjoy with her family outside.
  • She has been planing to go to her local lake with her family, because of her son's birthday, since the weather it's been pretty bad lately, she is not sure whether to go out or not.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather like today?
  • How long is the rain going to last?
  • What kind of clothes should I wear today?
  • Should I take an umbrella today?
  • How hot is it going to be?
